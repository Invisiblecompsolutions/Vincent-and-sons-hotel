About our background

VICENT AND SONS hotel is located in the heart of the historic center of Kampala in an extremely characteristic, quite and lively area within short walk distance to all sites and is surrounded by the extraordinary beauty of churches, buildings, shops and monuments.VICENT AND SONS hotel is part of a the most wanted and toured sites in Uganda.

On entering this charming hotel in Kampala, you will immediately sense its special intimate atmosphere that makes you feel like being in your own florentine home. Each detail has been passionately chosen and each room deserves a visit

VICENT AND SONS hotel style mixes valuable antiques and original artworks with an unexpected eclectic contemporary twist

Feel the best of the pearl of Africa

The entire House recalls the ancient ‘UGANDAN’ of times past where young Ugandans aristocrats lived while discovering the beauty and the artistic mysteries of Kampala city. .

The special charm and the cosy mood of VICENT AND SONS hotel will make you feel a home away from hme.
